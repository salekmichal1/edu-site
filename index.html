Plans

Recommended for start if you want to learn from time to time

This plan is perfect for learners who want to dip their toes into the world of online education. With access to select courses and limited features, the Basic plan is an affordable option for those on a budget.

The Premium plan offers full access to all courses and features on the platform. This plan is ideal for learners who want to explore a variety of subjects and achieve their goals quickly.

This plan offers advanced analytics and reporting features, as well as dedicated support from a EduCore representative and provide employees with access to high-quality online education.
